Welcome to Mount Fuji Visitor Center.  
We are delighted to have you here today.  
Please begin your visit at the information desk to receive a free map and guide.  
The observation deck on the second floor offers a fantastic view of the mountain.  
A short documentary film about Mount Fuji’s history is shown every 30 minutes.  
Feel free to explore our exhibition area which features traditional artifacts and photographs.  
For safety, please do not climb beyond the marked trails.  
Our café is open from 9 a.m. to 5 p.m., serving local sweets and drinks.  
If you have any questions, our multilingual staff is happy to assist you.  
We hope you enjoy your visit and make unforgettable memories!
